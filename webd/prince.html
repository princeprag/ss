MY COLLEGE - IIT GUWAHATI

IIT Guwahati, this is indeed not just an engineering college or a place in the map of India but a heaven, heaven in terms of its culture & climate. Yup, the uncertainity in the climatic conditions here can drive one literally crazy. It is common here that you might feel the scorching heat of the Sun here at afternoon with no hopes of relief but soon be blessed with the showers of pleasure and excitement in the evening. Being a high altitude area the pleasent cool wind that blows through the campus in the evening takes all our anxiety and problems and fills us with a feeling of high hope and energy. We do a wide range 0f tasks in IIT Guwahati. From running like lunatics after the small clues & hints in the most awaited event of freshmen year - the SCAVENGER HUNT to dancing like never before in the auditorium during the pro-nites of techniche,we have done hilarious things that we never even expected to do in an engineering college.

Feeling Fascinated????
If not you will feel soon when I will take you to the visual trip of IIT Guwahati. We will be seeing the important places here and some amazing pics that will make you even more fascinated to join this college...so here we go:

Academic & Administrative Building

The central most important building located in the heart of IIT Guwahati that is the center for various academic and administrative affairs. There are students affairs ,faculty affairs and many more like this there that regulates the proper functioning of all the activities in IIT Guwahati

Computer & Communication center AND Library

The site for the whole network and data services of the college. It is the best place for the people of freshmen year to hang out,watching movies,studying the whole day & night during exams and for all settings of WIFI & LAN in their PCs. Just beside the CC lies the Library that has the huge collection of books, textbooks,novels,reference-books,journals,magazines.Also the library has an online repository consisting of various e-journals,e-books research papers and many more of these. Most of the time you may not find a large population there but this is the most crowded place at the time of exams.

Bhupen Hazarika Audiotorium

From hosting the institute level orientation to the splending club orientations,this is the place where we got to know that 1+1=2 also ha sa proof,, Sounds stupid!!!!....Right???...But its true..Maybe you will also hear it if you join IIT Guwahati:). The audi is one of the most significant places in the campus...be it enjoying the pro-nites of the first big event of the college-Techniche or watching the stage plays of Xpressions- the Drama club or hooting in the name of hostels in various orientations...this is the place where you will make millions of memories with your friends.

Sports Ground

This is the place where you will be practising hard when the sun goes down if you are an sports enthusiast...be it the dedicated days of hard work for ones hostel at the time of Spardha- the Inter hostel sports fest or the times when you are having your summer camps practising hard day and night to win the gold for your IIT..this is one of the place that will remain close to your hearts forever...

Serpentine Lake

Ahhhh!!!!.. I wish I would have been in a position to illustrate about the beauty of this lake from my experiences watching the clear water having the reflection of the midnight moon with the cool breeze flowing accross. But the 7:1 figure have kept these dreams of me and many others like me only dreams till date...sed lyf!!!

Khoka Market

This is the best place to hang out with fraands after the hefty 8-5 schedule of the day and fuel ones body with the crispy samosas, chat, gol-gappe, burger, sandwitch and much more....surely a place where you will create hundreds of memories... Khoka chaaaal yaaar!!!!

ViewPoint

Okk!! Want to see the whole of IIT Guwahati from one place???? the beauty of the nature,,the buildings,, the lakes,, the mighty brahmaputra river...If yes... this is the place...from where you can experience yourself being on the top..the so-called narrow...rounded roads that finally takes you to the apex called the viewpoint will leave you with the extreme pleasure of witnessing the most beautiful screeneries...But beware TIGER ALERT!!:)
